## 🔐 VPN Troubleshooting Commands / Steps

### Basic Checks
- Verify internet connectivity
- Check VPN credentials
- Restart VPN client

### Advanced Checks
- Check active sessions in firewall
- Restart VPN service
- Verify required ports (e.g., 443, 1194)

### Network Reset (User Side)
- Flush DNS
- Restart adapter
- Reconnect VPN
